Objective: Vrinda store wants to create an annual sales report for sales 2022. So that, Vrinda can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2023.

Sample Questions.
1.Compare the sales and orders using single chart.
2.Which month got the highest sales and orders?
3.Who purchased more men or women in 2022.
4.What are different order status in 2022?
5.List top 10 states contributing to the sales?
6.Relation between age and gender based on number of orders.
7.Which channel is contributing to maximum sales?
8.Highest selling category?, e.t.c

Steps:
1.Data Cleaning- Checking null and duplicate values.
2.Data processing- Making Age Groups and monthname column.
3.Data Analysis- Above Queries


Insights:
1) Most of the sales were happened in March.
2) Women has done the most amount of sales in comparison to men.
3) Talking about order status, Most of the orders were delivered.
4) Top 10 states who has contributed most in sales are: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh,
                                                       Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Delhi, Kerala,
                                                       West Bengal, Andhra Pradesh, Haryana
5) In Adult category, women has done the most shopping.
6) Most sales is done in Amazon, then Flipkart then Myntra
